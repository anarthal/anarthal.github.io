resultset::resultset (4 of 4 overloads)

Constructs a resultset object by taking ownership of a view.

Synopsis 
resultset(
    resultset_view v);
Exception safety

Strong guarantee. Internal allocations may throw.

Object lifetimes

*this lifetime will be independent of v's (the contents of v will be copied into *this).

Complexity

Linear on rows and metadata size for v.
